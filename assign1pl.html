Eleven

The actual Wiki

Eleven is a fictional character played from the hit Netflix series Stranger Things. Portrayed by British actress Millie Bobby Brown, Eleven's character quickly gained worldwide recognition following the success of the series. Eleven serves as a protagonist for the series, possessing telepathic/ psychokinetic powers.

Fictional character biography

Season 1

Eleven is first depicted as being on the run, following her escape from the Hawkins Laboratory. While on the run, she meets Mike Wheeler, Dustin Henderson, and Lucas Sinclair, who are searching for their missing friend, Will Byers. After noticing the "011" tattoo on her forearm, the group begins referring to her as Eleven. Eleven spends the season using her powers to help the boys locate Will, who is in the Upside Down. She also begins to develop a romantic relationship with Mike.

Season 2

Eleven spends the majority of the season discovering more information about her identity. Out of fear for her safety, Police Chief Jim Hopper decides to move her into a cabin in the woods, isolated from everyone. Eleven spends her time developing her powers and becoming more controlling over them. She eventually grows tired of being kept in the cabin and runs away to visit Hawkins, which results in an argument between her and Hopper. She meets new student Max, who she initially thinks is flirting with Mike which angers her. Eleven finds out she has a mother and a sister this season, resulting in her going out to meet them.

Season 3

Eleven and Mike start the season as an official couple, which bothers Hopper. Eleven eventually dumps Mike after she finds out he is lying to her to get out of having to spend time with her. As a result, Eleven begins spending more time with Max and the two hang out at the new mall in Hawkins. Eleven spends the season fighting the Mind Flayer, whom she eventually defeats but at the loss of her powers. Hopper is presumed to be dead by the end of the season, resulting in the Byers offering to take custody of Eleven.

Development

Eleven's character was written to be based off of survivors from the MKUltra experiments. Other influences to her character influde E.T. and the animes Elfen Lied and Akira. Eleven was initially supposed to die in the first and only season, but following the success of the show, Netflix wanted a second season which resulted in the brothers having to keep the character alive. Millie Bobby Brown was chosen for the role because of her ability for convey emotions and her talent to portray a supernatural character.

Reception

Brown's performance as well as the character were subject to critical acclaim. Eleven quickly became the star of the show, inspiring a cult following and becoming a staple of modern pop culture. Brown's performance gained her two nominations for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series at the 69th and 70th Primetime Emmy Awards. She won the 2017 MTV TV Award for Best Actor in a Show, the Saturn Award for Best Performance by a Younger Actor, and a Kids Choice Award for Best Female Actor in 2018 and 2020.
